About Course


Computer Science Information Technology deals with design, implementation, and management of information system of both software and hardware processes. Computer Science is a scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications. CSIT deals with the design and development of new software and hardware parts of computer, implementing and managing computer-based information systems including software applications and computer hardware. The latest syllabu prescribed by the affiliating university makes the students ready to take up any task relating to the above.
